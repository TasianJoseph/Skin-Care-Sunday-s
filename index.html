About Skin Care Sunday's

This website is collated by the Code First Girls Janvi, Jehan, Sandra and Tash! We wanted to create this blog because we wanted to share our knolwedge about skin care and elaborate on the natural ways in which this can be achieved by using ingredients you probably buy in your weekly supermarket shop! Additionally, we wanted to create a safe space for young women in particular to explore ways that they can improve the health of their skin. We realise this is especially important because of the effects of pollution and hard water on our skin in the UK!

Skin Care Sunday's Promises:

  • That this blog will cover all of your skin care needs.
  • To provide a safe place to explore different solutions to your skin care problems.
  • Find you natural solutions to your skin care problems!
  • Post helpful recipes, tutorials and links to products or brands who will help progress your skin care journey.
  • To be responsive to your feedback, suggestions and queries.
  • Provide you with EXCLUSIVE discounts and bargains!

Almost all of our recipes and product suggestions are vegan friendly which means that everyone can enjoy our blog to it's fullest! On occassions where honey, milk or any other animal produce is present in a recipe, we will make suggestions for appropriate substitutions.
